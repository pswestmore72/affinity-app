WHAT IS AFFINITY?

Affinity is a tabletop roleplaying game about mortality, risk, and beautiful fragility. You play adventurers in Aeralon, a world where the dead rise, the gods war, and every choice has weight.

This is YOUR story. The dice are your life. Guard them well.

────────◇────────
THE FUNDAMENTAL TRUTH
────────◇────────

There are NO hit points.

You have Hit Dice — actual individual dice that exist or get destroyed.

Example:
• You have 5d10 body dice = five separate d10 dice
• Take 1 damage, fail defense = lose 1 die → now you have 4d10
• You don't subtract points. You lose DICE.

When your dice run out, you face death. This is the core of Affinity.

HOW TO ROLL
────────◇────────

There are two types of rolls: Action Rolls and Defense Rolls.

You'll need: Multiple d6s (two colors recommended) + polyhedral dice (d4, d6, d8, d10, d12, d20).

ACTION ROLLS (d6 Dice Pools)

When you attack, cast spells, or use abilities, roll d6s and count successes.

The threshold depends on the action's tier:

[TABLE - Use minimal gray style with gold left-accent bar]
┌─────────────┬───────────────┬──────────────────────────────────┐
│ Action Tier │ Threshold     │ What Counts                      │
├─────────────┼───────────────┼──────────────────────────────────┤
│ Basic       │ 2+            │ 2,3,4,5 = 1 success; 6 = 2      │
│ Advanced    │ 3+            │ 3,4,5 = 1 success; 6 = 2        │
│ Expert      │ 4+            │ 4,5 = 1 success; 6 = 2          │
│ Legendary   │ 5+            │ 5 = 1 success; 6 = 2            │
└─────────────┴───────────────┴──────────────────────────────────┘

At Level 1, most actions are Basic (2+ threshold).

A roll of 6 ALWAYS counts as 2 successes, regardless of tier.

Example: Basic attack, roll 3d6, get 6, 3, 2 → That's 4 successes.

DEFENSE ROLLS (Armor/Body Dice)

When you defend, roll your actual armor or body dice (d4, d6, d8, d10, d12, d20).

Defense always uses the same threshold: 5+

• Roll 5+ = Success — die survives, 1 damage cancelled
• Roll 1-4 = Fail — that die is destroyed

· · ─────────────────────── · ·
DEFENSE DICE SURVIVAL
Roll 5+ to survive. Bigger dice = better odds.

d4   0%  — Absorbs one hit. Always destroyed.
d6  33%  — Light. Fragile protection.
d8  50%  — Medium. Coin flip survival.
d10 60%  — Heavy. Built to last.
d12 67%  — Shields & magic. Hard to break.

Your armor is your lifeline. Invest wisely.
· · ─────────────────────── · ·

Example: You have 2d8 armor. Take 2 damage, roll both → 7, 3 → One survives (7 ≥ 5), one destroyed (3 < 5). You now have 1d8 armor.

ACTION RESOLUTION (The Core Loop)
────────◇────────

Every action in Affinity follows this pattern:

1. Declare what you're doing (and its tier — usually Basic at L1)
2. Roll your d6s (Proficiency dice + any exertion)
3. Count successes (based on the action's threshold)
4. Check requirements (did you meet the required successes?)
5. Apply effect

AT LEVEL 1

• Proficiency = 1 (you roll 1d6 for most actions)
• Most actions are Basic: 2+ threshold, need 1 success
• That's it. Roll 1d6, get 2+ = you succeed.

────────◇────────
EXERTION (Risk for Reward)
────────◇────────

Want more dice? Exert. But there's risk.

• Add extra d6s to your roll (up to Proficiency per action)
• If you roll a 1 on an exertion die: Lose 1 HD from your pool

At Level 1 (Proficiency 1): You can exert 1 extra die per action.

Example:
• Base roll: 1d6 (Proficiency)
• Add exertion: +1d6
• Roll 2d6, get 6, 1 → 2 successes, but lose 1 HD!

────────◇────────
Exert. Survive. Fracture.
────────◇────────

COMBAT BASICS

Your Turn in Combat

Movement: 10 yards (doesn't cost an action)

Actions: You get 2 actions per turn
• Attack, use ability, defend, help an ally, etc.
• Most things at Level 1 are Basic actions (easy to do)

Free Actions: Drop something, say something brief

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
PAGE 4 - ATTACKING & DEFENDING
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

ATTACKING
────────◇────────

1. Roll Proficiency d6s (+ any exertion)
2. Need: 1 success at 2+ threshold (for Basic attacks)
3. Extra successes: +1 damage each (capped at Proficiency)

At Level 1: Roll 1-2d6. Any die showing 2+ counts.

Example:
• Attacking with a sword (Base 1 HD damage, Basic tier = 2+)
• Roll 2d6 (Prof 1 + Exert 1), get 5, 3
• Both are 2+ → 2 successes → hit for 1 base + 1 bonus = 2 HD damage
• Bonus damage capped at Proficiency (max 1 at Level 1)

────────◇────────
DEFENDING
────────◇────────

When you take damage:

1. Attacker declares damage (e.g., "3 HD damage")
2. You roll defense dice from outermost layer (armor first, then body)
3. 5+ = that die survives, damage cancelled
4. 4- = that die is destroyed, move to next die

AT LEVEL 1

• Defense Capacity = 1 (you get 1 defense roll per attack)
• Roll 1 defense die. If 5+, cancel 1 damage. If not, lose that die.
• Any remaining damage = auto-lose dice from your pool.

Example:
• Take 3 HD damage, Defense Capacity 1
• Roll 1d8 (armor) → 6 = success, cancel 1 damage, armor survives
• 2 HD remaining, no rolls left → auto-fail → lose 2 armor dice
• If armor runs out, start losing body HD

HIT DICE TRACKING

Your character sheet shows dice pools:

Body HD:  ⬡ ⬡ ⬡ ⬡ ⬡  (5d10 body dice)
Armor:    ⬡ ⬡         (2d8 armor dice)

When you lose dice, cross them off. When they're gone, they're gone.

At 0 Body HD: You're in trouble (see Resolve).

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
PAGE 5 - RESOLVE & REST
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

RESOLVE (Mental Fortitude)
────────◇────────

Everyone starts at Resolve 5. It rarely drops at Level 1.

When Resolve drops (rare at L1):
• Taking large damage amounts in one round
• Using maximum exertion repeatedly
• Failing difficult actions with consequences

Don't worry about Resolve yet. Your GM will tell you when to check.

At 0 Body HD AND 0 Resolve = death. Until then, you can be saved.

────────◇────────
RESTING
────────◇────────

Rest (30 min - 8 hours, threatened):
• Regain 1 HD (at Level 1)
• Regain 1 Resolve
• Max 2 rests per day

Recover (6+ hours, safe place):
• Regain ALL HD
• Regain ALL Resolve
• Restore armor

PAGE 7 - THE THREE CLASSES (COMBATANT)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

► COMBATANT
────────◇────────

Hit Die: d10 (Tough)
Custodial Connection: The Concordant Halls — Keepers of Oath

You are purpose made manifest. Your weapon is an extension of your oath. The battlefield bends to those who know why they fight.

STARTING ABILITIES (Level 1)

Weapon Strike (Basic, 2+)
• Weapon attacks are Basic (2+)
• Required: 1 success
• Damage: Weapon base + extra successes (max +Prof)

Rally Cry (Basic, 2+)
• Required: 1 successes
• 30 yards, choose one ally
• Target gains +1d6 on their next action (doesn't burn HD on 1s)
• Usable Proficiency times per short rest

Hold the Line (Advanced, 3+)
• Required: 2 successes
• Duration: Proficiency rounds
• 10-yard zone around you: enemies cannot move through without contesting you
• Allies in zone gain +1d6 to their first defense each round

Oath-Driven Strike (Advanced, 3+)
• Required: 2 successes
• Weapon Strike + contest (your Prof dice vs their Prof dice)
• If you win: push 5 yards OR knock prone
• Usable Proficiency times per short rest

ARMOR & WEAPONS
• Light armor (2d6)
• Medium armor (2d8)
• All weapons

WAY OF THE BLADE (Level 2)
Choose this Way at character creation. You gain your first node at Level 2.

Justice, enforcement, righteous consequence.

Row 1: Honed Edge — Weapon Strike deals +1 HD against targets at half HD or less.

PATH: WAR-TRAINED
Your Primary Feat, chosen at Level 1.

You have extensive martial training and battlefield expertise.

Benefit:
• +1d6 to all attack rolls when wielding your signature weapon type (choose: blades, axes, hammers, spears, or bows) — doesn't burn HD on 1s
• +1 HD to your Body pool permanently
• When you reduce an enemy to 0 HD in combat, restore 1 HD to yourself (adrenaline surge)

Proficiency 3+ Milestone: When fighting alongside allies, your signature weapon attacks gain an additional +1d6 (doesn't burn HD). War is taught in formation; you've mastered it.

PAGE 8 - THE THREE CLASSES (RANGER)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

► RANGER
────────◇────────

Hit Die: d8 (Balanced)
Custodial Connection: The Living Accord — Masters of Life and Death

You are the wilderness made flesh. You track what others cannot find. The wild recognizes you as kin.

STARTING ABILITIES (Level 1)

Hunter's Strike (Basic, 2+)
• Weapon attacks are Basic (2+)
• Required: 1 success
• From concealment or height: +1 HD damage

Mark Prey (Basic, 2+)
• 60 yards, one creature
• Required: 1 success
• You gain +1d6 to all actions targeting marked creature
• You know direction and distance to marked creature
• Lasts until target drops to 0 HD or you mark another
• Usable at will

Killing Ground (Advanced, 3+)
• Required: 2 successes
• 30-yard radius zone, Proficiency × 2 rounds
• Choose one effect:
  - Hazard: 1 HD damage per round to enemies
  - Concealment: Light cover for allies
  - Mire: Difficult terrain for enemies
• Usable Proficiency times per short rest

Culling Strike (Advanced, 3+)
• Against Marked Prey only
• Required: 2 successes
• Weapon Strike at +Prof damage
• Target must be at half HD or less
• Usable Proficiency times per short rest

ARMOR & WEAPONS
• Light armor (2d6)
• Medium armor (2d8)
• All weapons, favors ranged/versatile

WAY OF THORN (Level 2)
Choose this Way at character creation. You gain your first node at Level 2.

Predation, competition, ambush mastery.

Row 1: Predator's Patience — From concealment or height, +2 HD damage instead of +1 (Hunter's Strike upgrade).

PATH: WILD-TOUCHED
Your Primary Feat, chosen at Level 1.

You have a deep connection to nature and beasts.

Benefit:
• +2d6 to all Wilderness skill rolls (doesn't burn HD on 1s)
• You can communicate simple concepts with animals (intent, not mind-reading)
• Once per long rest, call a Beast Companion (wolf, hawk, bear) that aids you for one scene. It has HD = half your level (rounded up), acts on your turn following simple commands

Proficiency 3+ Milestone: Your Beast Companion gains +1d6 to its own actions. Your animal communication deepens — you can convey complex plans like "guard this passage and howl if anyone approaches from the east."

PAGE 9 - THE THREE CLASSES (WEAVER)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

► WEAVER
────────◇────────

Hit Die: d6 (Fragile)
Custodial Connection: The Curator Arcanum — Masters of Knowledge

You bend reality. You reshape what others call fixed. Fragile, but devastating.

STARTING ABILITIES (Level 1)

Spell Strike (Basic-Expert, varies)
• Spellcasting uses spell tier thresholds
• Known Spells: Proficiency × 3 (3 at Level 1)
• Prepared Spells: Proficiency + 1 (2 at Level 1)
• Ritual Casting: Any Arcane spell, even if not known

Weave Sight (Advanced, 3+)
• Required: 1 success
• Duration: Proficiency × 10 minutes
• See magical auras, concentrations, effects within 60 yards
• Identify spell types (Basic = domain, Advanced = specific spell)
• Usable at will

Spell Shaping (Passive)
• When casting area spells, exclude up to Proficiency creatures from effect
• Represents precision control

Arcane Recovery (Passive)
• Once per long rest, during short rest: regain Prof HD
• Meditation channels ambient magic to restore vitality

ARMOR & WEAPONS
• No armor proficiency (can't wear armor effectively)
• All weapons (rarely used)

STARTING SPELLS (Choose 3 from Arcane list below)
• Magic Missile (Basic, 2+) — 30 yards, auto-hit, 1 HD force
• Flame Bolt (Basic, 2+) — 60 yards, 2 HD fire
• Shield Ward (Basic, 2+) — Self, next attack -1d6, reaction
• Mage Armor (Basic, 2+) — Self, 8 hours, grants 2d6 armor
• Detect Magic (Basic, 2+) — 30 yards, sense magic for 10 min

WAY OF UNLEASHED FORCE (Level 2)
Choose this Way at character creation. You gain your first node at Level 2.

Evocation mastery, raw magical power.

Row 1: Empowered Cantrips — Basic tier damage spells deal +1 HD.

PATH: SPELL-TOUCHED
Your Primary Feat, chosen at Level 1.

Magic flows through you more readily than most.

Benefit:
• Learn +Proficiency bonus spells from Arcane domain (doesn't count against Known limit) — +1 spell at Level 1
• Ritual casting time reduced by 25%
• Once per long rest: Overcast — cast a spell at your current tier, but apply the effect of one tier higher (damage, duration, range). Still roll at your tier.

Proficiency 3+ Milestone: When you cast a spell and spend exertion, one of your exertion dice becomes safe for that roll (doesn't burn HD on 1s). Your magical discipline minimizes waste.
