# Army Leaders
## Commander Cards for Affinity: Flux

*Your Leader defines your army. Choose wisely.*

---

## Overview

Your **Army Leader** is the most important card in your deck:
- Starts the game on the battlefield
- Provides an Affinity discount (cards matching your Leader's Affinity cost 1 less)
- Has passive and activated abilities that shape your strategy
- **If destroyed, you lose the game**

Leaders are not included in your 1000-point budget—they are chosen separately.

## Memory Leaders

### Aluneth's Herald
*"The tides remember what you have forgotten."*

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| **Affinity** | Memory |
| **Health** | 25 |
| **Attack** | 2 |
| **Defense** | — |

**Passive — Tidal Memory:**  
At the start of each round, look at the top card of your discard pile. You may put it on the bottom of your deck.

**Activated — Call the Tides** (3 Source):  
Return target card from your discard pile to your hand. It costs 2 less this turn.

*"She speaks with Aluneth's voice. The rivers answer."*
