### COMBAT REFERENCE

**Initiative:** Roll Awareness or relevant skill

**Defense Roll:** Proficiency dice + Exertion (if chosen)

**Success Thresholds:**
- **Basic:** 2+ successes
- **Moderate:** 3+ successes  
- **Advanced:** 4+ successes (same as Moderate in current rules)
- **Expert:** 4+ successes
- **Legendary:** 5+ successes (6s only count)
- **Ultimate:** 6+ successes (6s only count)

**Dice Rules:**
- Roll 6 = 2 successes (explosive)
- Roll 1 on exertion die = burn 1 HD
- Proficiency dice don't burn HD on 1s

### QUICK REFERENCE

**Healing During Combat:** Use class abilities or potions  
**Healing During Short Rest:** Restore Proficiency HD  
**Healing During Long Rest:** Restore all HD and Resolve  

**Collapse at 0 Resolve:** Each round, roll 1d6:
- 5-6 = stabilize for 1 round
- 1-4 = lose 1 HD

**Death at 0 HD:** Character dies (or makes death saves per GM ruling)
