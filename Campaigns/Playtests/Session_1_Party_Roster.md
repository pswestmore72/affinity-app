## Character 1: Kael Thornbrook (Ranger, Level 2)

**Player:** [GM-Controlled Playtest]

### Core Stats
- **Current HD:** 5d8 (5 HD available)

- **Armor Temp HD:** 2d8 (Medium Armor - Scale Mail, rolled fresh each rest)

### Defining Affinity
**Virtue:** **Decay** *(Darthun)* ‚Äî *"Accept necessary endings or clear corruption to allow renewal"*

Kael understands that forests burn to feed new growth, that rotting trees become soil for saplings, that predators cull the weak to strengthen the herd. He is pragmatic about death and endings‚Äînot cruel, but accepting that some things must end for others to begin.

**Affinity Dice:**
- **Gain:** 1 Affinity Die when accepting necessary endings or clearing corruption (max 1 per day at Prof 1)
- **Store:** Max 2 Affinity Dice (Level 2)
- **Spend:** Add to any roll as exertion dice (burn HD on 1s, count toward action exertion cap)
- **Currently Stored:** 0

### Background & Personality

**Age:** Late 20s (human)  
**Appearance:** Weathered features, calloused hands, dark hair tied back, practical leather and furs, carries himself with quiet confidence. Has a scar across his left cheek from a bear encounter years ago.

**Personality:**
- **Pragmatic and direct** - Doesn't waste words, says what needs saying
- **Respectful of nature's cycles** - Understands death is part of life
- **Protective of his companions** - Will accept his own ending to ensure theirs continues
- **Quietly thoughtful** - Speaks little but observes everything

**Background:**
Kael grew up in a logging community that learned the hard way about overcutting. When his village depleted their forests and sickness spread through the starving land, he watched entire families leave or die. A wandering Ranger taught him that nature demands balance: take without giving back, and the wild reclaims its debts.

He's traveled since, hiring out as guide and tracker. Recent work brought him near Stonebridge when the tremors started. He's seen sealed chambers before‚Äîalways bad news when mortals crack them open.

**Motivation for The Hollow Below:**
"Something ancient has been disturbed. If it's not sealed properly, more than a mine collapses‚Äîthe whole town dies. I've seen it before. Won't let it happen again."
