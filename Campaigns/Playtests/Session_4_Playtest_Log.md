## Session 4 Playtest Analysis

### Mechanics Tested

‚úÖ **Paths System:**
- Each character gained Novice tier in chosen Path (1/3 progress)
- Paths felt meaningful and thematic (Torvan = Sentinel, Kael = Tracker, Lyra = Ritualist)

‚úÖ **Gear Restoration (No Gold):**
- Torvan's armor destroyed twice (combat consequence)
- Replaced during Recover with no gold cost (assumed availability)
- **Feels:** Appropriate‚Äîarmor breaks, towns have smiths, heroes get equipped
- Removes bookkeeping, focuses on adventure

‚úÖ **Puzzle Challenges:**
- Theological cipher (Archive door) rewarded lore knowledge
- Meditation Crypt floor trap rewarded pattern recognition
- Vault guardian test rewarded roleplay and virtue demonstration
- **Feels:** Balanced between combat and problem-solving

‚úÖ **Diplomacy in Combat:**
- Party convinced Obsidian Arbiter mid-combat (Persuasion + Religion checks)
- Ended boss fight through roleplaying rather than HP depletion
- **Feels:** Rewarding player creativity, not just dice pools

‚úÖ **Environmental Narrative:**
- Archive preservation magic (no decay, frozen time) created atmosphere
- Meditation Crypts trap floor built tension
- Vault simplicity (bare altar, three relics) focused attention
- **Feels:** Each area had distinct identity

‚úÖ **Sacred Relics Distribution:**
- Three items for three characters felt fair
- Each relic tied to temple theme (stability, memory, authority)
- No conflict over loot‚Äîthematic fit clear
- **Feels:** Rewards enhance character concepts, not just power

### Issues Identified

‚ö†Ô∏è **Path Advancement Pacing:**
- Gained only 1 point this session (1/3 progress)
- At this rate, reaching Adept (2 points) takes 2 sessions, Expert (3 points) takes 3 sessions
- **Recommendation:** Consider awarding 1 point per major milestone OR allow training during long downtime
- **Current Feel:** Slow but deliberate

‚úÖ **Boss Encounter Balance:**

- **Observation:** Arbiter would have been BRUTAL if forced to fight to 0 HD (would take 4-5 rounds minimum, severe attrition)

‚úÖ **Torvan Armor Depletion:**
- Lost shield + armor in 2 attacks (4 HD + 3 HD)
- Defense Capacity 1 at Prof 2 still very limiting
- **Feels:** Appropriate fragility‚Äîarmor is ablative, combat is dangerous

‚úÖ **Affinity Dice Not Used:**
- Party had combined 4 Affinity Dice stored
- Didn't spend any this session

- Relics as rewards felt thematic and earned

**DEFENSE CAPACITY FORMULA CHANGED (February 16, 2026)**

Based on Session 4 observations and analysis, the Defense Capacity formula has been updated systemwide:

**OLD FORMULA (used in Sessions 1-4):**  
Defense Capacity = ¬Ω Proficiency (rounded up, minimum 1)
- Prof 1-2: 1 roll per attack
- Prof 3-4: 2 rolls per attack
- Prof 5-6: 3 rolls per attack

**NEW FORMULA (starting Session 5):**  
Defense Capacity = Proficiency (full value)
- Prof 1: 1 roll per attack
- Prof 2: 2 rolls per attack
- Prof 3: 3 rolls per attack
- Prof 4: 4 rolls per attack
- Prof 5-6: 5-6 rolls per attack

**Impact on Current Party (Prof 2):**
- Torvan: 1 roll ‚Üí **2 rolls** per attack
- Kael: 1 roll ‚Üí **2 rolls** per attack
- Lyra: 1 roll ‚Üí **2 rolls** per attack

**Rationale:**
- Creates "blow for blow" combat feel (defending multiple times per attack = trading strikes)
- Doesn't create dice slog (most attacks 2-4 HD, most defenses 2-4 rolls = quick resolution)
- Clean math (no halving, no rounding)
- Rewards heavy armor investment (multiple rolls benefit layered defense)
- Still allows armor destruction (just takes 2-3 hits instead of instant overwhelm)

**Session 5 will test this updated formula.**
