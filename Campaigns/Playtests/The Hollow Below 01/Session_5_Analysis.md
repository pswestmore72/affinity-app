## 3. Burden System (New Mechanic Introduced)

### Divine Burdens = Trade Responsibility for Power

**Concept:** Khuldras offered each PC a "burden" (mechanical cost + narrative restriction) in exchange for permanent abilities.

**Torvan - Cornerstone Burden:**
- **Benefit:** Once per long rest, designate ally as "Foundation" â†’ they cannot be forcibly moved, knocked prone, or grappled while near you
- **Cost:** Cannot flee combat while Foundation endangered; if Foundation drops to 0 HD, lose 1 Resolve (burden of failure)
- **Gameplay:** Torvan designated both Kael + Lyra as Foundations during boss fight â†’ held position entire combat
- **Felt MEANINGFUL:** Cost created tension (Kael dropped to 0 â†’ Torvan lost Resolve), benefit saved Lyra from Earthquake Stomp

**Kael - Renewal Burden:**
- **Benefit:** When you witness/cause ending (death, decay), grant ally +1d6 to next action (doesn't burn HD)
- **Cost:** Must speak aloud what was lost + what will grow from it (narrative requirement); causing unnecessary death loses burden until atonement
- **Gameplay:** Kael transformed Obsidian Titan from regenerating enemy to fertile earth, spoke words of renewal
- **Felt IMPACTFUL:** Narrative requirement added drama ("From this decayâ€”RENEWAL!"), mechanical bonus helped party

**Lyra - Lorekeeper Burden:**
- **Benefit:** Once per long rest, perfectly recall any text/inscription/speech from past 24 hours; teaching someone grants them +1d6 when using that knowledge
- **Cost:** MUST record significant knowledge discovered; cannot Rest until documented (guilt prevents sleep)
- **Gameplay:** Lyra translated Marble Titan's phrases, taught Torvan tactical insight (granted him +1d6 bonus), documented everything in Testament
- **Felt THEMATIC:** Recording requirement drives Lyra's character (scholar archetype), bonus makes her useful beyond combat

### Burden Evaluation

**Strengths:**
- Costs are MEANINGFUL (not trivial penalties)
- Benefits feel EARNED (not just loot drops)
- Narrative requirements create roleplay hooks ("speak the words," "record the knowledge," "hold the line")
- Aligns powers with character archetypes (Guardian protects, Tracker transforms, Scholar preserves)

**Concerns:**
- GM enforcement required (what counts as "unnecessary death"? when is knowledge "significant enough" to require recording?)
- Lost burden = harsh penalty (if Kael murders innocent, loses Renewal Burden until atonementâ€”appropriate or too punitive?)
- Power level variance (Cornerstone Burden's defensive bonus very strong, Lorekeeper's utility less combat-impactful)

**Recommendations:**
- Add GM guidance section: "Burdens require adjudication. Narratively significant violations lose burden; minor infractions earn warnings."
- Clarify atonement process: Quest? Ritual? Time-based?
- Consider burden tier system (Novice burdens = minor cost/benefit, Master burdens = major trade-off)

## 4. Divine Compacts (New Mechanic)

### 1/Week Divine Intervention from Khuldras

**Mechanic:** Each PC can invoke Khuldras once per week for auto-success on foundation-related check (protection, transformation, preservation).

**Power Level:** VERY HIGH (auto-succeed = bypass all difficulty, no roll)

**Limitation:** 1/week (can't spam), must align with foundation principles (GM adjudication)

**Comparison to Affinity Dice:**
- Affinity Dice: +1d6 bonus, burn HD on 1s rolled, limited by storage (3 max)
- Divine Compact: Auto-succeed, no cost, 1/week
- **Compacts MORE powerful but LESS frequent** (appropriate for deity-tier reward)

**Balance Concerns:**
1. **Save Forever Problem:** Players may hoard Compacts indefinitely, never using them ("but what if I need it LATER?")
2. **GM Arbitration Heavy:** "Is this use foundational or selfish?" = constant judgment calls
3. **Trivializes Climactic Moments:** Auto-succeed on boss fight protection check = no tension?

**Recommendations:**
- Add: "Unused Compacts don't stack weekly" (use it or lose it, encourages spending)
- OR: "Khuldras grows distant if Compact unused for 1 month" (connection fades without invocation)
- Provide GM examples: "Foundational uses: protecting innocents from collapsing building, transforming corrupted land, preserving dying language. Selfish uses: winning arm-wrestling contest, getting free drinks, intimidating merchant."
- Consider adding narrative consequence for trivial use: Khuldras disappointed, Compact temporarily revoked (1 week penalty)

**Verdict:** Strong mechanic for patron-based campaigns (Paladins, Warlocks, Clerics), needs guidelines to prevent abuse âœ…

## 5. Multi-Solution Boss Encounters

- **Solution:** 3 successful Lore checks (Moderate 3+) to decipher Old Khuldric phrases, speak truth, pacify Titan
- **Result:** Titan kneeled, blessed Testament (+1d6 â†’ +2d6 upgrade), became guardian statue
- **NO HP damage dealt** (pure knowledge solution)
- **Felt REWARDING:** Intelligence + Memory affinity synergy showcased, combat avoided through scholarship

**Granite Titan (Torvan's Trial):**
- **Solution:** Provoke Titan to attack only Torvan (Influence check), survive sustained assault to prove endurance (Guardian's Mark + Cornerstone Burden)
- **Result:** Titan kneeled in respect after Torvan's declaration ("I'll outlast you, I'll outlast the WORLD!"), trial complete
- **Combat involved but victory = roleplay + mechanical commitment** (tanking damage to prove burden)
- **Felt THEMATIC:** "I am foundation" = perfect Guardian moment

**Obsidian Titan (Kael's Trial):**
- **Solution:** Reduce to 0 HD via combat, THEN use Renewal Burden to transform (Religion check + Affinity Die)
- **Result:** Titan crumbled to fertile earth, sapling emerged, trial complete
- **Combat + transformation** (hybrid solution)
- **Felt DRAMATIC:** Kael dropped to 0 HD channeling transformation, success despite collapse

### Multi-Solution Boss Design Lessons

**Strengths:**
- Rewards DIVERSE PC strengths (combat, knowledge, roleplay)
- Creates memorable moments (Lyra's scholarly victory felt as epic as Kael's combat DPS)
- Encourages creative thinking (players explored non-HP-depletion solutions)
- Aligns victory conditions with character archetypes (scholar uses lore, warrior uses endurance, ranger uses transformation)

**Implementation:**
- Give bosses **Trial Objectives** separate from HP (e.g., "Marble Titan pacified by demonstrating knowledge")
- Multiple victory conditions (HP depletion OR objective completion)
- Objective completion can END combat early (Marble Titan stopped attacking after 3 Lore successes)

**Recommendation:** Codify multi-solution boss design in GM Guide. Provide templates:
- **Combat Boss:** HP depletion only
- **Puzzle Boss:** Solve challenge to bypass/weaken (e.g., find phylactery before engaging lich)
- **Social Boss:** Negotiation/persuasion ends conflict (e.g., convince warlord to stand down)
- **Trial Boss:** Demonstrate virtue/skill to earn victory (e.g., prove worthiness to guardian)

## Balance Concerns Identified

**1. Bulwark's Presence Power Level**

**Issue:** Torvan's Adept ability grants +1 Defense Capacity to allies within 10 yards.

**Problem:**
- At Prof 2, allies go from Capacity 2 â†’ 3 (boss-tier defenses!)
- At Prof 6, allies would have Capacity 7 (higher than most bosses)
- Makes entire party significantly tankier just by standing near Torvan
- May trivialize combat difficulty scaling

**Options:**
- **Option A:** Cap bonus at +1 (doesn't scale with Proficiency) â† RECOMMENDED
- **Option B:** Change to "grant ally +1 success on ONE defense roll per attack" (not full Capacity boost)
- **Option C:** Limit to 1 ally within 10 yards (no party-wide buff)
- **Option D:** Make it cost 1 Resolve activation (not always-on passive)

**Recommendation:** Option A + monitor. If still too strong, implement Option D.

**2. Enduring Ritual Permanent Effect Exploit**

**Issue:** Lyra can make 1 ritual permanent per long rest on "object/location."

**Problem:**
- Current wording unclear: Can she cast permanent Magical Armor on herself? (infinite defense layer)

**Fix:** Add clause:
> "Permanent rituals on creatures (including self) last 24 hours maximum. Permanent rituals on objects or locations persist until dispelled."

**Result:** Prevents self-buff exploits, allows cool environmental uses (permanent ward on doorway, permanent light on statue).
