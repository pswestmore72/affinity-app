## Session 3 Playtest Analysis

### Mechanics Tested

‚úÖ **Rest System:**
- 8-hour Rest successfully restored all HD, Resolve, spell slots
- Clear, simple to track
- Felt meaningful after depleting resources in Session 2

‚úÖ **Recover System:**
- Torvan's armor restoration (6+ hours, Basic check) worked smoothly
- Required narrative commitment (time + safe space)
- Shield couldn't be restored (too damaged) ‚Äî felt appropriate
- **Observation:** Recover provides tactical choice (spend time to restore armor OR push forward depleted)

‚úÖ **Social Influence System:**
- Multiple Influence checks (Durg, Council report, leniency plea)
- Moderate 3+ and Advanced 3+ felt appropriate for social stakes
- Party coordination (multiple PCs contributing) felt natural
- **Success:** Convinced Council, cleared Durg, shaped policy

‚úÖ **Affinity Die Rewards:**
- Torvan: Guardian (protected Durg's spirit, served as defender)
- Kael: Decay (helped town accept endings, move toward renewal)
- Lyra: Memory (already earned in Session 2)
- **Felt earned, not arbitrary**

‚úÖ **Level Advancement:**
- Milestone system (3 sessions = Level 3) felt appropriate
- Proficiency 1 ‚Üí 2 increases power meaningfully:
  - Exertion budget: 1 ‚Üí 2 (double the tactical options)
  - More spells known/prepared (Rangers/Weavers)
  - Better success rates on checks
- **Observation:** Level 3 feels like "competent adventurer" tier

‚úÖ **Reward Structure:**
- Gold (500g split) ‚Äî practical
- Magic items (Runestone, Guard's Shield) ‚Äî thematic and useful
- Social rewards (reputation, free lodging, Council favor) ‚Äî meaningful
- **No +1 swords** ‚Äî stayed true to system philosophy

### Issues Identified

‚úÖ **Social Encounters Flow Well:**
- Party naturally divided responsibilities (Lyra lore, Torvan protection, Kael guidance)
- NPC threads (Durg, Kara, Harlan, Torvald) tied to character affinities
- **Recommendation:** More campaigns should include social/political sessions between dungeon crawls

‚ö†Ô∏è **Rest/Recover Clarity:**
- Rest (8h) vs Recover (6h) could be confused
- **Recommendation:** Add sidebar in rules clarifying: "Rest = restore resources, Recover = repair gear"

‚úÖ **Milestone Leveling Works:**
- 3 sessions = 1 level (for Levels 2-3) felt right
- Gave time to test mechanics before increasing complexity
- **Recommendation:** Slower leveling at higher tiers (5+ sessions per level at Levels 8+)
